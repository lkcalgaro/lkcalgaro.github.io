About


It can be said that Frice Pastry has been in the works for years as that's how long owner and pastry chef Alissa Frice has been working on her craft. Frice Pastry is the perfect combination of traditional French techniques blended with influences from around the world. Frice Pastry boasts pastry cases filled with breakfast pastries both sweet and savory as well as beautifully composed desserts.
